About the J8i clan chat

The j8i cc is an OSRS raiding community focused on mastering the Chambers of Xeric. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, we provide structured raid groups, coaching, and clan events.

Master the Chambers of Xeric

Join our clan and learn the most effective raiding strategies. From puzzle rooms to final boss mechanics, we ensure that every raider is well-prepared.

Clan Raiding Events

Participate in scheduled clan raids and showcase your skills in our leaderboards. Whether you're going for speed runs or pet hunts, there's a spot for you!
